Behaviour

Behaviour is composed of a DiscoveryBehaviour and an GossipsubBehaviour`. It automatically connect to newly discovered peer, except specified otherwise, and propagates intents to other peers.

IntentGossipEvent

IntentGossipEvent describe events received/sent in the gossipsub network. All information are extracted from the GossipsubEvent type. This type is used as a wrapper of GossipsubEvent in order to have only information of interest and possibly enforce some invariant.
